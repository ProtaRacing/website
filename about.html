What is Prota Racing?

Prota Racing is a North American F1 in Schools team from Lakeway, Texas

What is F1 in Schools?

F1 in Schools is a global challenge in which teams of students use CAD/CAM software to collaborate, design, analyze, manufacture, test, and ultimately race miniature compressed air powered cars milled from an F1 model block.
